How to Make a Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Ingredients

2 slices of bread

1 jar of peanut butter (preferably crunchy)

1 jar of your favorite jelly, jam, preserves, etc.

Recipe

1. Get the two slices of bread, the jar of peanut butter, and the jar of jelly or jam.

2. With a knife, spread a layer of peanut butter onto one side each of the slices of bread.

3. With a clean knife or a spoon, spread your jelly or jam across the layers of peanut butter.

4. Place one slice of bread, spread side down, on top of the other slice of bread, spread side up.

5. Cut diagonally across the sandwich, creating two triangular slices.

6. Enjoy! Keep a drink nearby if the peanut butter is too thick to swallow dry.
